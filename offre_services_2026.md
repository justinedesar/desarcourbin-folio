## FAQ

### 🇬🇧 Frequently Asked Questions

**What is a Fractional VP Sales?**
A Fractional VP Sales is a senior commercial leader who works with your company part-time or on a project basis. You get the expertise and impact of a seasoned VP Sales without the full-time salary and benefits cost — ideal for scale-ups that need senior leadership but aren't ready to commit to a full-time hire.

**How quickly can we start?**
Typically within 1–2 weeks after an initial scoping call. Engagements begin with a discovery phase to assess your current GTM setup and define priorities.

**Do you work with early-stage companies?**
My primary focus is B2B SaaS companies at Series B+ looking to scale. However, I also mentor and coach earlier-stage founders through my advisory and investment network.

**What industries do you cover?**
B2B SaaS & Tech, across EMEA. I have deep experience in ERP, cloud solutions, partner ecosystems, and enterprise software.

**Can you help us build an international team?**
Yes — I have built and led distributed teams across France, Spain, Ireland, UK, Benelux, and the Nordics. International expansion and cross-border team management are core strengths.
